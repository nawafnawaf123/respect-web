Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Digital Subscription

Respect Verification is a digital subscription. Once activated, the verified badge and related digital features may become available immediately inside the app.

2. Refund Requests

Refund requests are reviewed case by case. You may contact support if you purchased a subscription by mistake, experienced a technical issue, or were charged incorrectly.

3. Processing

Payments and refunds are processed through Paddle. If a refund is approved, Paddle will process the refund back to the original payment method according to its policies.

4. Abuse

We may refuse refunds in cases of abuse, fraud, repeated misuse, or violations of our terms.

5. Contact

To request help with a payment or refund, contact us at support@respect-app.com.
